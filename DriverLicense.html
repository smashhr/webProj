General Requirements for vehicle driving license:

1- The legal age is 18 for light vehicle and motorcycle licenses, and 21 for heavy vehicle and truces.

2- Applicant must be free of any disease that may impair their driving ability (some persons with certain disabilities may be subject to medical examination).
3- Vision test form approved by an authorized optician.
4- Two photos with blue backdrop.
5- Copy of the Identity Card for Omanis, Residence Card for expatriates.
6- Blood group form.
